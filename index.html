WELCOME TO HIMALAYAN CAFE

Himalayan cafe is a neighborhood cafe located on Asematie 1, Vantaa, serving locally-roasted coffee along with sandwiches, salads, fresh-squeezed juices, smoothies, and pastries. The lower garden district space has coffee shop standards like free Wifi and local art, but a few unexpected gems set it apart-beer, wine, Irish coffee, mimosas, and magazines/books.
